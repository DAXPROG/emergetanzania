Organization Background

Emerge Tanzania (Emerge) is a Non-Governmental Organization, registered under the NGO act no 24 of 2002 as amended from time to time with its headquarters in Morogoro region located at Area Six, Kichangani Ward and its Postal Address, P.O. Box. 1484, Morogoro. The organization’s activities are carried out in Tanzania mainland.

The overall goal of Emerge Tanzania is to promote a holistic and sustainable community development through capacity building and advocacy for deprived and marginalized women and children in Tanzania. The focus target community of Emerge are deprived and marginalized women and children who have limited access to health services, quality education, entrepreneurship skills, and economic development.

Emerge Tanzania is an equal and nonpartisan and not discriminating body and shall not support its target population in line with race, religion, ethnicity and political orientation.

Vision

Deprived and Marginalized women, Youth and children in Tanzania capacitated for a perpetual and vibrant society.

Mission

To promote a holistic and sustained community development through capacity building and advocacy for deprived and marginalized women and children in Tanzania.

Core Values

1. Proactive, 2. Accountability, 3. Integrity, 4. Gender sensitive, and 5. Commitment. .

Objectives

1. To promote good governance, environmental conservation, climate change and technology innovation for sustainable development

2. To mobilize resources and receiving contributions from donors and supporters for supporting vulnerable community

3. To promote income generating activities (IGA) to families to afford education costs.

4. To promote protection level of economy for families under provision, promotion and production for economically well being

5. To support HIV affected families on prevention, care and treatment and responding to pandemic diseases
